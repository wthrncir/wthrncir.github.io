Acerca de este proyecto

William Hrncir

8th Grade Student, Lanier Middle School

Hi! I'm William, and I created this website as part of my 8th-grade curriculum at Lanier Middle School. I have a passion for coding and learning languages, so I decided to combine both interests to build this interactive Spanish learning platform. My goal is to make learning Spanish fun and accessible for my fellow students through engaging games and activities.

The IB Service as Action Project

This website was developed as an International Baccalaureate (IB) Service as Action project. Service as Action is a core component of the IB Middle Years Programme (MYP), which encourages students to make a positive difference in their communities and the world.

Through this project, I aimed to serve the Lanier community by providing a helpful resource for Spanish students. By creating digital tools for vocabulary, grammar, reading, and writing practice, I hope to support my peers in their language learning journey.
